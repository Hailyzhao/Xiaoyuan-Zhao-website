When I was twelve, I immigrated to America. I am a person who likes to talk a lot but in America, nobody talked to me because I did not speak English. I turned into a quiet person, but I began to develop an interest in mathematics. Math is a language that the whole world is fluent in. This is the only subject I was best at. The only disadvantage I have in math are word problems. When I first learned about word problems, my grades and I both became depressed. I was terrified that I even have to give up on math, my favorite subject. I lost hope. My grades did not go back up and I knew this was not me. I knew I am a person who could get back up after a fall, but I could not this time. Good thing I met an outstanding and responsible teacher that was willing to help me go through all my hardships. She would use simple words and body language to communicate. Although my grades still did not reach up to my expectations, I was satisfied with my progress. Throughout high school, my math had maintained. Because of this, I decided that I would like to study business in the future. Business itself is a calm job and it will allow me to concentrate on one thing at a time.
